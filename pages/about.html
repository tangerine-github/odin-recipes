About

Well, how do I know what I want? To help answer that question, here is some basic info about cases, pcbs, switches, and keycaps.

Cases

Theres a lot of layouts to choose from. Refer to the image below to figure out what you want. There are also different case materials, but its usually ABS plastic or aluminum.

PCBS

There isn't much to discuss. If it didn't come with the kit, just pick one that is compatible with your case, and choose between soldered or hotswap.

Switches

Too much to talk about lol. There is linear, tactile, and clicky. Just get linears.

Keycaps

There are ABS and PBT keycaps. There are also different profiles, but just get ABS Cherry lol.
